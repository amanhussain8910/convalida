Salesforce Cloud CRM Solutions

Convalida's Salesforce Consulting team has rich experience of implementing solutions across multiple industry verticals. We help our customers in identifying and defining use cases, designing a solution, delivering a POC and deploying the solution.

Our CRM consultants can help in following areas:

  • Salesforce Cloud Solutions - Our solutions equip companies with optimized processes with seamless implementation, integration, and management of the Salesforce Cloud. We help organizations gain deeper insights for smarter decision-making by implementing Analytics and connecting sales, service, and marketing for integrated operations.
  • Salesforce Implementations - Our Salesforce consulting services focus on hassle-free CRM implementation after evaluating existing business procedures and validating the readiness for recommended solutions. As a certified Salesforce CRM partner, we help companies streamline diverse processes through phased approach-based or one-shot Salesforce implementation as per organizational needs.
  • Integration and Data Migration - Having a dedicated Salesforce CRM consulting team with expertise in rendering custom integration services, we are recognized as one of the top Salesforce consultants. We aid companies to choose the most potent integration tools, methodologies, and best practices for integrating Salesforce effortlessly.
  • Salesforce Application Development - Our Salesforce technical consultant team specializes in developing APIs and custom solutions to extend the functionality of the CRM platform. We help organizations in the smooth adoption of Cloud CRM with optimized fields, objects, processes, workflows, and data management.
